## ✨ Overview

Saju (사주, 四柱) is a traditional Korean astrology system rooted in the Chinese Four Pillars of Destiny. It analyzes a person's birth year, month, day, and hour to reveal personality traits, life patterns, and fortune.

This project brings that ancient wisdom into a clean, accessible modern web experience.

- 📅 **Birth date & time input** — Enter your four pillars (year, month, day, hour)

## 🌏 About Saju (사주)

Saju is one of Korea's most enduring cultural traditions. The "four pillars" represent:

| Pillar | Represents |
|--------|------------|
| 年柱 (Year) | Ancestry, early life |
| 月柱 (Month) | Parents, youth |
| 日柱 (Day) | Self, marriage |
| 時柱 (Hour) | Children, later life |

Each pillar is expressed through the 10 Heavenly Stems (천간) and 12 Earthly Branches (지지), forming a unique cosmic fingerprint.
